Bio

Behind the Chair

Abbie is a highly experienced hairstylist with 10 years behind the chair, specializing in precision haircutting, custom hair color, and extensions. Known for creating beautiful, personalized results for clients of all ages, she has built a loyal, full clientele over the years, many of whom have become like family.

While her everyday guests are at the heart of what she loves most, Abbie has also had the privilege of working beyond the salon in editorial and fashion settings. Her experience includes magazine work, model shoots, reality television personalities, fashion icons, and even award-winning opera performers.

Whether she’s creating a lived-in blonde, a seamless extension transformation, or the perfect signature cut, Abbie brings creativity, technical expertise, and genuine passion to every guest who sits in her chair.
