Behind the Chair

Hi, I’m Madison! I’m a wife, a mama of two, and someone who truly loves helping women feel confident and beautiful. Behind the chair, my goal is to create a space where you can relax, feel comfortable, and leave feeling like the best version of yourself. I specialize in color, cuts, and extensions, and I especially love creating effortless, lived-in looks that fit your lifestyle and still make you feel put together. Whether you’re wanting a big transformation or just some time to unwind and refresh yourself, I love building genuine relationships with my clients and making every appointment feel personal.
