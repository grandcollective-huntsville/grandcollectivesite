Bio

Behind the Chair

Back in 2010, I left behind my favorite desert, El Paso, Texas, and made Huntsville, Alabama my home. Shortly after that, in 2012, I set my roots here by starting my cosmetology career and finding my own sense of community in what used to be our small town.

These days I use words my kids aren’t allowed to hear while I make my way down 231, enjoy baking and spending my off days outside, and convince my children the lake is actually the real beach because, let’s be frank, I’m not driving five-plus hours to go there.

I love creating blended and lived-in colors for my ladies, or gentlemen. Frankly, I don’t mind either way: as long as your hair will fit in a foil, I’ll make it work. I have to admit something though, extensions are truly where my heart is. Being able to give my clients their confidence back is what keeps me going into work every single day.

If you’re looking for a stylist who’s a bit more laid back when it comes to personal style yet still delivers every time in absolutely slaying your hair, I’m your girl. Come as you are. You’ll be accepted and appreciated every single time.
