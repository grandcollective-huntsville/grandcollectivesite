Bio

Behind the Chair

Hi, I’m Reshae! A wife, mom of two amazing girls, and a stylist apprentice who found her way into this industry through life experience and a lifelong love for hair. As a mom to two daughters with long hair, I’ve spent years learning hair care at home before ever stepping into a salon professionally.

I entered the salon world as a receptionist, helping behind the scenes with inventory and daily operations. Abbie saw my passion early on and encouraged me to take the leap into an apprenticeship. Since January 2026, I’ve been apprenticing, growing my skills daily, and falling more in love with this craft. I’m so grateful to be part of a supportive salon environment where I can turn something I’ve always loved into a career, and I can’t wait to have you in my chair.
