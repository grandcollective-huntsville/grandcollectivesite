Bio

Behind the Chair

Hi, I’m Reshae! I’m a wife, mom of two amazing girls, and a stylist who truly found her way into this industry through life experiences. For as long as I can remember, hair has always had a way of coming back into my life. Even before stepping into a salon professionally, I spent years learning how to manage and care for long hair at home, especially with my two daughters.

Being a mom is my greatest joy, and it’s at the heart of everything I do. I was raised by my dad, so learning those “girly” things like hair didn’t come naturally at first. I was lucky to have my childhood friend and stylist, Jess Joiner, take the time to teach me how to care for my own hair. That experience stuck with me and planted a seed I would come back to later in life.

After reconnecting with Abbie, who I originally knew from school and later through the Tennessee Valley Vipers, an opportunity opened up for me to step into the salon world as a receptionist. While managing inventory and helping behind the scenes, something clicked. Abbie saw my passion and growth early on and encouraged me to take the leap into an apprenticeship.

In January 2026, I officially began my journey as an apprentice. Every day I’m learning, growing, and falling more in love with this craft. I’m so grateful to be in a space where I’m supported, encouraged to be myself, and able to turn something I’ve always loved into a career. I can’t wait to have you in my chair.
